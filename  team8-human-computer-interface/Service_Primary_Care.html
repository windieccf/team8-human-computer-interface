Primary Care


We provide primary care and specialize in family medicine.

Our services range from management of acute and chronic illnesses, immunization, health screening and vaccination, medical check-up, minor procedures and treatments, x-ray and laboratory services.

We are equipped with the following modern facilities:

• 7 General Consultation Rooms
• 3 Specialist Rooms
• 1 Counselling Room
• 1 Physiotherapy Room
• 1 Treatment Room
• 1 Isolation Room
• Pharmacy
• Laboratory
• X-Ray (Tue/Wed/Thu: 9am - 11.30am / 1.30pm to 5pm)

Pharmacy

All Undergraduates and Postgraduates matriculating are required to complete their medical examination before entering the university. The screening will be performed at the University Health Centre.

Candidates who accepted the offer from Medicine, Dentistry or Nursing must also be screened for immunity to Hepatitis B, Chickenpox and Rubella. .
