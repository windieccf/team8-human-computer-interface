Important Informations

Pharmacy

All Undergraduates and Postgraduates matriculating are required to complete their medical examination before entering the university. The screening will be performed at the University Health Centre.

Candidates who accepted the offer from Medicine, Dentistry or Nursing must also be screened for immunity to Hepatitis B, Chickenpox and Rubella. .
